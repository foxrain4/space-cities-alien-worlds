Space Cities & Alien Worlds

High tech, low certainty.

Even with bots and starships, the unknown can’t be contained.
New planets bring awe—and terror. Think teamwork, AI companions, and environments beyond human understanding.

🚀 What Defines This World Type?

  • Spaceships, domed cities, abandoned outposts.
  • Encounters with alien life, natural or intelligent.
  • Advanced tech clashing with primal fears.
  • Isolation, both physical and emotional—far from Earth and safety.

🛰️ Worldbuilding Tips

  • Design alien ecosystems that feel believable yet uncanny.
  • Create factions—corporations, AI guilds, explorers, rogue bots.
  • Let the environment shape the story: gravity, atmosphere, climate hazards.
  • Mix sterile futuristic tech with unpredictable organic life.

👽 Narrative Hooks

  • Your ship responds to an ancient distress signal that shouldn't exist.
  • An AI begins to question its own orders—and yours.
  • A colonist goes missing. The planet says it never existed.
  • You wake from cryosleep with a new memory that isn't yours.

🎮 Game Design Ideas

  • Exploration zones with environmental threats and resource limits.
  • AI-driven companions with changing personalities.
  • Base-building and survival mechanics in alien climates.
  • Logs, scans, and discoveries that piece together a planetary history.
The stars promise wonder—but also silence, secrets, and solitude. Build a future that questions what it means to be human.
